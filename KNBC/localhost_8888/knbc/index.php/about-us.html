About Us

This is Us

 

Welcome!

Welcome to the website of KNBC Island Supply your one stop shop for all your cottage and recreational supplies.

We have a seamless coordinated approach to enhancing your cottage experience. Everything from docks, bunkies and firepits to lumber, cedar rails, barn beams and firewood all in one location.

 

About Us

KNBC Island Supply is a locally owned and operated corporation in Central Frontenac and Prince Edward County, Ontario.

We distribute high quality, Canadian made and hand-crafted products that are very versatile and long lasting. We are passionate about supporting local small businesses and adding to the life and success of our community.

 

Mission Statement

To provide our customers with high quality locally made products and raw materials.

Our focus is to provide a seamless integrated experience. We reduce our carbon footprint while supporting local and Canadian businesses. We choose to supply the highest quality products that provide beauty, style and longevity. Our products will enhance the enjoyment and add value to your home or cottage.
